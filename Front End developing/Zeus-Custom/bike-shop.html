Fatherhood of Zeus Custom

Siva - whose real name is Sivananthan Subbramaniam, - is the owner and founder of Zeus Custom,a India-based custom motorcycle shop that looks as though it was designed by a steampunk architect and a handful of hipsters.

Ceo & Founder - Siva


NEW ARRIVALS

Ask A Zeus Custom If You Need A Unique Motorcycle. “All of KTM bikes are unique, because I like creating something new,” says Siva, whose motorcycle aesthetics transcend the machines, demonstrated by his brown desert boots, tight denim jeans and casual plain t-shirt. “People bring in their bikes and say: 'I've seen a bike you've customised; I want mine to look the same. " And then I tell them: 'I'll make you a unique bike; no copy.’”
